TeensConnect FAQ

Welcome to the official TeensConnect FAQ!

Things you should know or asked stuff!

What is TeensConnect?

TeensConnect is a website for teenagers or children to connect to same-minded people!

How to access TeensConnect?

To use TeensConnect, you have to register an account on the homepage. Press "Register" on there, and you're good to go!

Will my child be safe here?

We try to make a safe and comfortable environment for everyone. We also filter out bots and fakes to prevent most bad things that could happen.

Do I have to share personal data?

Yes. To create a safe environment for everyone, we can't let anyone secretize their real identity. Also, we will need to verify your age before you can start.

We will never...

We will never ask you about your payment information, bank connections or anything. This website is free and will stay free.

We are NOT...

We are strictly NOT a dating site. Of course, if you really think you found your special someone here, we can't block it. But dating is NOT the main purpose of this site.

What account data will be saved?

For others to find you, all of your public information can be seen by anyone. You're contact data will be saved, but not shared. This data will be deleted when the account gets deleted

Account Restrictions

You need to be 12+, which will be verified. Otherwise, there's no restrictions! Just be yourself!

Account Deletion

Accounts get deleted on Inactivity, Rule Breaks, and requested Deletions You can request Account Deletion in your Profile Settings.

Rules

We all use our real identity here. Nicknames are allowed, but the real name should be correct.

No inappropiate content (Pictures, Messages), which is for any type of inappropiate content.

Fake Age, Fake Name, overall Bots, will be deleted without warning

Note that chats are annonymous. Chats will only be visible for us if one of the chatters reports a message. Reporting will send us the last five messages.
